- "Datadog platform demo"
- "New Relic monitoring platform video"
- "GitLab DevOps platform intro"
- "HashiCorp infrastructure product video"
- "Y Combinator startup pitch 2 minutes"

**Key style elements:**
✅ Speaker on camera (you)
✅ Cut to screens/graphics while you continue talking (voiceover)
✅ Text overlays appear at key moments
✅ Picture-in-picture option (small speaker window + large screen)
✅ Fast-paced cuts (2-5 seconds per visual)
✅ Professional background music (low volume)

## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Screen Recordings:
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD) minimum
- **Frame rate:** 30fps or 60fps
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264) or MOV
- **Duration:** 3-10 seconds each
- **Audio:** Muted (your voice will be main audio)

### Static Graphics:
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Format:** PNG (for transparency) or JPG
- **Text:** Large, readable fonts
- **Colors:** Match your brand colors

### Photos:
- **Resolution:** High resolution (at least 1920x1080)
- **Format:** JPG
- **Orientation:** Landscape (horizontal)
